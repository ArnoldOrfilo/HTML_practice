I bought this car on the second day I arrived UK. At first I was planning to buy a BMW M4 from the same dealer nearby London, but after seeing and test driving this car, I bought it without hesitation.
Though it looks aggressive, but it is actually a very comfortable-driving car, the suspension and seats are softer than I thought, and it react gently when I'm pressing down on the gas pedal. However, as I switch to sport mode, it suddenly turns into a different car, the reaction is much quicker than driving mode.
In conclusion, this is actually a driver-friendly car, it feels like e-class while driving in the drive mode, but it can turn to an animal when I want to have some fun, which is very good for daily drive. Most important is that it sounds way better than a M4.
