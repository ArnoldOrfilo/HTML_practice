This is a very old car, it was manufactured in 2004. It has a amazing 5.5 bi-turbo v12 engine, which allow this 2-ton heavy car accelerate to 100km/h within 4.4 seconds.
At the beginning, it has a lot of problems with the circuits and engine parts due to the heat of it's v12 engine. I fully rebuilt the engine and most of the circults and made it fresh as new. it's interior is in a oldschool vibe but still very comfortable.
Unlike the Maserati, this car is quiet while driving, but it actually has more horsepower than the Maserati, and it feels like I'm sitting on a sofa while driving it, and the suspension is so soft that it feels like I'm driving a boat.
Except some potential issues that could happen due to the age of this car and the heat that the engine creates, this car is a perfect combination of comfortbility and horsepower.
